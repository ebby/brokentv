Frequently Asked Questions
Wait...so what is this?
XYLO is a product inspired by TV, aspiring to be your home for leanback storytelling. This is our first stab at creating something valuable, but we have a lot to learn. You're engaging in the inception of an idea that we hope will evolve into something important to our lives.

What's up with that logo?
It's a xylophone, get it?

What's the deal with these channels?
Currently we're running a few test channels. We have a full structure for anyone to operate a channel. If you're intested in a custom branded channel, contact info@xylocast.com.

Why can't I skip?!
Because we're curious to see how that impacts viewing behavior. This will morph as we learn from our users, stay tuned.

Does everyone see the same content?
Yes, it's always in sync so you can instantly watch along with friends. But you can manually play any video from the sidebar.

What happens when I pause?
Your video pauses, and can be resumed at anytime. The timeline will progress forward and you can hop back in sync by clicking 'GO LIVE'.

Who can tell that I'm online?
Only your Facebook friends.

Who can see my profile?
Your Facebook friends and anyone who links to you from a reply (i.e. a friend of a friend) or a related tweet.

Where is the content coming from?
YouTube publishers that we find interesting. To suggest content, scroll to the end of the timeline and paste a YouTube link.
