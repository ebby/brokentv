Frequently Asked Questions
Wait...so what is this?
This is a product inspired by TV, aspiring to be your home for leanback storytelling. This is our first stab at creating something valuable, but we have a lot to learn. You're engaging in the inception of an idea that we hope will evolve into something important to our lives.

Who can tell that I'm online?
Only your Facebook friends.

Who can see my profile?
Your Facebook friends and anyone who links to you from a reply (i.e. a friend of a friend) or a related tweet.

Where is the content coming from?
YouTube publishers that we find interesting. Users also contribute to content by searching for a liking videos
