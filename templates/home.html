About
Company

Xylo is a new media lab, building platforms to change how we interact with stories. We believe our voice, as users, should influence what we watch and how we watch it. The programs we see on TV should be as relevant as our latest tweet. By applying everything we've learned from the social web to traditional media, Xylo aspires to bring the world's best stories into one theater.

Products

Our product, XyloCast, is currently in private beta. We also work closely with select media companies to provide value from our platform and product experise. Publishers looking to engage their audience on unprecendted levels should contact us.

Team

Our team is firmly rooted in great technology and storytelling, and we're always excited to mix the two. We are located in Brooklyn, NY.

To get in touch, please email info@xylocast.com
